Before your visit

How to prepare.

✦ Booking Instructions

  • Always check the link in bio for available appointments first
  • Don't see your date? DM me with the time in mind — I may be able to fit you in
  • Please do not DM asking what dates are available without checking the booking app first

✦ Before Your Appointment

  • Come with a clean, freshly washed face and lashes
  • Avoid moisturizers, SPF, or facial creams until after your service
  • Remove contact lenses before your appointment
  • Eat before your appointment and wear comfortable clothing
  • Shower beforehand — you cannot shower for 24hrs after lash application
  • Silence your phone 💕

After your appointment

Lash aftercare.

💧

Avoid getting lashes wet for the first 24–48 hours

🚫

Do not rub or pull your lash extensions

🧴

Clean lashes daily with an oil-free lash cleanser, fan dry on cold, brush with spoolie

💄

No mascara or oil-based products on your lashes

😴

Avoid sleeping on your face to preserve your set

📅

Stay on top of infills every 2–3 weeks for best results
